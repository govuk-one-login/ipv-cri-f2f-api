Go to a Post Office to finish proving your identity with GOV.UK One Login

Dear John Doe,

Your Post Office customer letter was sent in the same envelope as this cover letter.

You need to go to a Post Office that offers in-branch verification on or before the date shown on your customer letter.

If you do not do this, you’ll need to start proving your identity again.

What you need to take to a Post Office

When you go to a Post Office, you must take:

  • your Post Office customer letter
  • the photo ID shown on your Post Office customer letter

Which Post Office to go to

Your customer letter shows which Post Office you chose to go to.

You can go to a different Post Office as long as it offers in-branch verification: https://www.postoffice.co.uk/identity/in-branch-verification-service#bf-full-width

At the Post Office

A member of Post Office staff will:

  • check your customer letter
  • scan your photo ID
  • take a photo of you

You do not need to contact the GOV.UK One Login team if you decide to go to a different Post Office.

After you’ve been to the Post Office

You’ll get an email about the result of your identity check. You’ll usually get it within a day of going to the Post Office.

If you need help

Contact the GOV.UK One Login team:

https://home.account.gov.uk/contact-gov-uk-one-login

Regards,

GOV.UK One Login team
